2. Clear Browser Cache for This Site

If the app still doesn't work, try clearing your browser cache:

  • Chrome: Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete (or Cmd+Shift+Delete on Mac), select "Cached images and files", and click "Clear data".
  • Firefox: Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete (or Cmd+Shift+Delete on Mac), select "Cache", and click "Clear Now".
  • Safari: Go to Safari menu > Preferences > Advanced, check "Show Develop menu in menu bar", then go to Develop > Empty Caches.
